How
to invest in afic

If you're just starting out, becoming a shareholder is easy.

You need an account with a broker before you can start buying AFIC shares. There are two types of brokers to suit different investment styles: full-service brokers and online brokers. You can visit the ASX website for a list of recommended brokers.

Full-service stockbroker

A dedicated full-service stockbroker will typically offer a broad range of services including general advice. Full-service stockbrokers may charge a higher brokerage fee.

Online broker

Online brokers are non-advisory share-trading platforms that give you access to buy and sell shares online.
